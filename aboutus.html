About Us

We are in the process of creating a website called “Symptoms Quiz” for the ISP. Our goal is to compile the most common illnesses and conditions that people may experience. This platform will help the community understand their symptoms and encourage them to take proactive steps for their health.

How Does It Work?

Our website guides users through a series of questions about their symptoms to help identify potential conditions. Each question is dynamically generated based on the user’s previous responses, ensuring a personalized and accurate experience tailored to their specific needs.
